Vendor Agreement
Version: 1.0
Document Type: Contract Summary

Overview
This agreement summary outlines standard rules for termination, payment timing, and confidentiality obligations between the company and an approved vendor.

Termination
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.
Immediate termination is allowed in the event of material breach, fraud, or violation of confidentiality obligations.

Termination Clarifications
The standard termination notice period is 30 days in writing.
Either party can end the agreement by giving 30 days written notice.
A party may terminate the contract immediately if the other party commits fraud.
Immediate termination is also permitted for a material breach of the agreement.
A violation of confidentiality obligations is grounds for immediate termination without waiting for the ordinary notice period.

Payment Terms
Invoices are due within 15 business days of receipt unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Payment Clarifications
Payment is required within 15 business days after an invoice is received.
The invoice payment term is net 15 business days from receipt.
Invoices must be paid no later than 15 business days following receipt unless the parties agree to different written terms.
The standard vendor payment window is 15 business days.

Confidentiality
Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of shared proprietary information for 3 years after termination.

Confidentiality Clarifications
Confidentiality obligations survive termination for 3 years.
Shared proprietary or confidential information must remain protected for 3 years after the agreement ends.
The post-termination confidentiality period is 3 years.
The duty to keep shared proprietary information confidential continues for 3 years following termination.
